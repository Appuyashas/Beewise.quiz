🛡️ How the Anti-Cheat System Works
1. Tab Switch Detection — The browser detects every time a student switches to another tab or app during a quiz. Each switch is counted and saved with the result.
2. Question Shuffling — Every student gets a different random order of questions AND shuffled answer options, making copy-pasting useless.
3. Data shown below — Only students with 1+ tab switches appear here. Zero means no switching detected.
